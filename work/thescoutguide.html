The Scout Guide

Originating out of Charlottesville, Virginia, the Scout Guide is a curatorial publication that launched its initial web presence in 2012 with Business Bullpen. I served as the frontend developer on the first two design editions of the website. The nationally distributed publications and website serve as both a beautiful and valuable resource for travelers, designers, foodies, fashionistas and more.
