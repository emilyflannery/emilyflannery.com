About Emily

I am a designer and front-end web developer with a passion for finding elegant, clean and sophisticated solutions for my clients' print and web needs.

After earning a BA in fine art and political science from Sewanee: The University of the South, I began working as a project manager and junior art director at Flannel, a Vermont and California-based branding and web development agency. It was there, as the understudy to a talented creative director, that I married my eye for design with the technical skills of web development and learned how to transform a website from artistic vision to seamless reality. I began freelancing in 2011 and am based out of Austin.

Collaborating with my clients is part of what I love most about my job — listening to their ideas and expectations and delivering solutions that meet and exceed their goals. I am a perfectionist, and I strive to deliver products that look and function exactly as they were intended, no small detail overlooked. I thrive on challenging my creative and technical limits and I draw inspiration from, really, everything around me.

I love good music, good books, good food, and a good conversation. I value a healthy, holistic approach to life.
